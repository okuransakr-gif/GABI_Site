What is GABI?

Generative to Agentic AI Business Incubator

GABI is not just a chatbot. It's a full AI system that acts — moving from generating ideas to autonomously executing the steps needed to build a real startup alongside you.

The name tells the story: Generative AI produces — ideas, plans, content. Agentic AI acts — it takes structured actions, coordinates specialized agents, and drives outcomes. GABI bridges both worlds to build your company.

The Challenge

Six walls blocking Lebanese entrepreneurs

Lebanon's crisis created a structural void. GABI is designed to close all six gaps simultaneously.

01
Ideation Gap
No structure to validate raw ideas against real market data and local constraints.
02
Capability Gap
Lack of technical and business skills to move from concept to executable plan.
03
Resource Gap
No access to capital, tools, or infrastructure to build and launch a product.
04
Execution Gap
Can't navigate from idea to product without a clear roadmap and technical support.
05
Market Gap
No channel or strategy to reach customers locally or across the MENA region.
06
Psychological Gap
Economic collapse and social pressure have eroded the confidence needed to try.
